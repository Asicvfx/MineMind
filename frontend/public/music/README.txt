MineMind — Background music for MoodMode
=========================================

Drop 3 royalty-free MP3 files here with these EXACT names:

  focus.mp3   — calm lo-fi / jazz (for Focus mode)
  chill.mp3   — soft groove / chill beat (for Chill mode)
  chaos.mp3   — hype / energetic beat (for Chaos mode)

The game works fine without them — music just won't play.
useGameAudio.ts loads these gracefully (no crash if missing).

WHERE TO GET FREE (royalty-free, no copyright) TRACKS
------------------------------------------------------
1. Pixabay Music     — https://pixabay.com/music/  (free, no attribution)
     search: "lofi", "chill", "hype" / "drum and bass"
2. FreePD            — https://freepd.com/  (public domain)
3. Incompetech       — https://incompetech.com/  (Kevin MacLeod, CC-BY — credit him)
4. Chosic            — https://www.chosic.com/free-music/all/

RECOMMENDED PICKS
-----------------
focus.mp3  → Pixabay search "lofi study" or "calm jazz" — pick something ~2-3 min
chill.mp3  → Pixabay search "chill lofi groove"
chaos.mp3  → Pixabay search "energetic" / "phonk" / "drum and bass"

Keep files reasonably small (<3 MB each) so the page loads fast.
After adding files, just refresh the browser — no rebuild needed.
